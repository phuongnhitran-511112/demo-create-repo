Stops 80% of annoying cold sales & marketing emails

Professionals get a lot of emails, and most spend their days sorting unsolicited cold sales and marketing emails from important messages in their inbox. DPM automatically detects, labels and archives these annoying emails so you don’t have to.

Your new blacklist

We’ve developed advanced detection technology that automagically archives and labels these emails as you receive them. But nothing is perfect. If a pesky email gets through our defenses, every user has their own Blacklist to report and stop inbox invaders in their tracks.

Easy as adding a label

DPM works your work Gmail account across all devices, and with the Chrome Plugin, comes with handy buttons you can use inside of the Gmail interface.
But if you’re on a different email client or on mobile. Just add/remove the @Pitches label from any email and we’ll take care of the rest!

Always On!

DPM is always on 24/7, stopping unwanted emails from invading your inbox. We constantly update our filtering technology to ensure we are doing our absolute best to keep your inbox free of cold sales and marketing emails.
